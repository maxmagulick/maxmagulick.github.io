# Part 1 тАФ Product Architecture

**Create a week of content in 90 minutes using AI.**

A workflow system for beginner creators, busy professionals, and new personal brands.

> This is not a course. This is not a prompt pack. It is a repeatable system you run every week.

---

## 1. Complete Product Architecture

### The one-sentence promise
You sit down once a week, follow a fixed set of steps with AI doing the heavy lifting, and walk away 90 minutes later with a full week of content ready to post.

### The problem it solves
Most creators don't quit because they lack ideas. They quit because content is unpredictable, unscheduled, and starts from a blank page every single time. Creator AI OS removes the blank page and the guesswork.

### How the system is built (4 layers)

| Layer | What it is | Why it exists |
|-------|------------|---------------|
| **1. The Engine** | A repeatable 5-step weekly workflow | So you never start from zero |
| **2. The Inputs** | Your profile, audience, and content pillars (set up once) | So the AI sounds like *you*, not a robot |
| **3. The Prompts** | Copy-paste prompts for each step of the engine | So you don't have to "know how to prompt" |
| **4. The Home Base** | A Notion workspace that holds everything | So nothing gets lost and you can repeat it forever |

### The Engine: the weekly 90-minute run

This is the heart of the product. Every week, you run the same five steps.

1. **Set the week (10 min)** тАФ Pick your theme and which platform(s) you're posting to.
2. **Generate ideas (15 min)** тАФ Use the Idea prompts to produce 10тАУ15 content ideas from your pillars.
3. **Write the content (35 min)** тАФ Turn the best ideas into captions, hooks, and scripts with the Writing prompts.
4. **Polish and format (20 min)** тАФ Edit for your voice, add CTAs, format per platform.
5. **Schedule and file (10 min)** тАФ Drop everything into your Content Calendar and mark it ready.

That's the whole product in motion. Everything else exists to make these five steps fast and easy.

### Design rules (what we will NOT do)
- No theory. Every page leads to an action.
- No "AI literacy" lessons. Users copy, paste, and tweak.
- No 40-video course. The shortest path to a posted week wins.
- No tool overwhelm. One AI chat tool + one Notion workspace. That's it.

# Part 3 тАФ The 90-Minute Content Sprint

**Goal:** Create a week's worth of content in 90 minutes using AI.

A simple, repeatable run you do once a week. Six phases, a timer, and copy-paste AI prompts. No experience needed.

---

## Before you start (2-minute setup)

- Open one AI chat tool (ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini тАФ any free version works).
- Open your Notion workspace (or a blank doc).
- Set a 90-minute timer.
- Decide your **week's theme** in one sentence (e.g. "beginner budgeting tips").

> Realistic target: **5тАУ7 posts** for one main platform. Doing more than one platform? Use Phase 5 (Repurposing), don't double the work.

### The 90 minutes at a glance
| Phase | What | Time |
|-------|------|------|
| 1 | Idea Generation | 10 min |
| 2 | Research | 15 min |
| 3 | Outline | 10 min |
| 4 | Content Creation | 35 min |
| 5 | Repurposing | 10 min |
| 6 | Scheduling | 10 min |
| | **Total** | **90 min** |

---

## Phase 1 тАФ Idea Generation

**Goal**
Get 10тАУ15 content ideas tied to your weekly theme. Pick the best 5тАУ7.

**Time estimate:** 10 minutes

**Exact steps**
1. Paste this prompt into your AI tool:
   > "I'm a creator posting about **[theme]** for **[who it's for]**. Give me 15 short-form content ideas. Make them specific, beginner-friendly, and scroll-stopping. One line each."
2. Read the list. Star the 5тАУ7 that feel easiest and most useful.
3. Drop the chosen ideas into your Research Hub / doc.

**Common mistakes**
- Trying to invent ideas from scratch instead of letting AI batch them.
- Picking "clever" ideas over clear, useful ones.
- Overthinking тАФ spending 30 minutes choosing. Pick fast.

**AI tools used:** ChatGPT / Claude / Gemini (chat).

**Expected output:** A shortlist of **5тАУ7 chosen ideas** for the week.

---

## Phase 2 тАФ Research

**Goal**
Gather just enough facts, angles, and examples so each idea has substance.

**Time estimate:** 15 minutes

**Exact steps**
1. For your 5тАУ7 ideas, ask AI in one go:
   > "For each of these ideas, give me 3 key points and 1 simple example a beginner would understand: **[paste ideas]**."
2. Skim the output. Delete anything that feels generic or wrong.
3. Quick-check any surprising facts with a single web search.
4. Paste the keeper points under each idea.

**Common mistakes**
- Falling into a research rabbit hole (this is 15 minutes, not 15 tabs).
- Trusting AI facts blindly тАФ sanity-check numbers and claims.
- Collecting way more than you'll use.

**AI tools used:** Chat tool; optional web search / Perplexity for fact-checks.

**Expected output:** **3 key points + 1 example per idea**, lightly verified.

---

## Phase 3 тАФ Outline

**Goal**
Turn each idea + its points into a clear skeleton (hook тЖТ body тЖТ call to action).

**Time estimate:** 10 minutes

**Exact steps**
1. Ask AI:
   > "Turn each idea below into an outline: a strong hook, 3 bullet points, and a one-line CTA. Keep it simple. **[paste ideas + points]**."
2. Check each hook тАФ does it make you want to keep reading? If not, ask for 3 alternatives.
3. Save the outlines; this is your writing roadmap.

**Common mistakes**
- Skipping outlines and writing blind (slows Phase 4 way down).
- Weak hooks тАФ the hook is 80% of the post; don't rush it.
- Making outlines too detailed; they're skeletons, not drafts.

**AI tools used:** Chat tool.

**Expected output:** **5тАУ7 outlines**, each with hook + 3 points + CTA.

---

## Phase 4 тАФ Content Creation

**Goal**
Write the full posts from your outlines, in your voice.

**Time estimate:** 35 minutes (the biggest block)

**Exact steps**
1. Feed your outlines in batches:
   > "Write these as **[platform]** posts using the outlines below. Tone: **[5 words for your voice]**. Short sentences, no jargon, keep my CTA. **[paste outlines]**."
2. Read each draft out loud in your head. Fix anything that doesn't sound like you.
3. Tighten the first line of every post (the hook does the work).
4. Add personal touches: a story, an opinion, or a specific number.
5. Mark each finished post as **Ready**.

**Common mistakes**
- Posting raw AI text тАФ it sounds generic. Always edit for voice.
- Editing forever. Aim for "good and done," not perfect.
- Forgetting the CTA (tell people what to do next).

**AI tools used:** Chat tool; optional Grammarly for a quick polish.

**Expected output:** **5тАУ7 finished, ready-to-post pieces**.

---

## Phase 5 тАФ Repurposing

**Goal**
Stretch your best 1тАУ2 posts into extra formats for other platforms тАФ without writing from scratch.

**Time estimate:** 10 minutes

**Exact steps**
1. Pick your 1тАУ2 strongest posts.
2. Ask AI:
   > "Turn this post into: (a) a short video script, (b) a 5-slide carousel, (c) an X/Twitter thread. Keep the core message. **[paste post]**."
3. Light-edit each version and file it with the rest.

**Common mistakes**
- Trying to repurpose everything тАФ only repurpose your best.
- Copy-pasting the same text across platforms (each format needs a small tweak).
- Skipping this phase тАФ it's the cheapest way to double your output.

**AI tools used:** Chat tool; optional Canva for carousel visuals.

**Expected output:** **2тАУ4 bonus pieces** in new formats.

---

## Phase 6 тАФ Scheduling

**Goal**
Lock every piece to a day so the week is fully planned (and ideally auto-posts).

**Time estimate:** 10 minutes

**Exact steps**
1. Open your Publishing Calendar (or a simple weekly list).
2. Assign each post a day and time. Spread them out тАФ don't dump all on Monday.
3. If you use a scheduler (Buffer, Later, Metricool тАФ free tiers work), paste posts in and queue them.
4. No scheduler? Set phone reminders for each post day.
5. Mark each item as **Scheduled**.

**Common mistakes**
- Finishing content but never assigning dates (so nothing gets posted).
- Bunching everything on one day.
- Ignoring best post times for your platform.

**AI tools used:** Buffer / Later / Metricool (schedulers). AI optional: "Suggest a 7-day posting schedule for these posts on [platform]."

**Expected output:** **A fully scheduled week** тАФ every post has a date.

---

## You're done

In 90 minutes you went from a blank page to a **scheduled week of content**:
- 5тАУ7 main posts
- 2тАУ4 repurposed bonus pieces
- All assigned to days and queued

**Make it a habit:** same day, same time, every week. The second sprint is always faster than the first.

### Quick troubleshooting
- **AI sounds robotic?** Give it 5 tone words and 1 example of your writing.
- **Ran past 90 minutes?** You researched too long (Phase 2) or edited too long (Phase 4). Time-box harder next time.
- **Stuck for ideas?** Reuse last week's best post as a new prompt: "Give me 10 spin-offs of this."
