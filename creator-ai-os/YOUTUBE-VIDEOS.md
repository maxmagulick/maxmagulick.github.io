3. **Step 1: Ideas (1:30–3:30)** — Use a simple AI prompt to generate 15 ideas; pick the best 5.

5. **Step 3: Write (6:00–10:00)** — Draft posts with AI, then edit one to sound human. Show the before/after.

8. **Mistake 7 (9:15–10:15)** — Only making one post per idea instead of repurposing.
